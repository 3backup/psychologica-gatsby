# Psychologica - Renata Zuba

Psychologica is the public website for Renata Zuba, a psychologist and psychotherapist in Rzeszow, Poland. The site describes psychological support, psychotherapy, psychoeducation, supervision, location details, and contact paths for people seeking help.

- Psychotherapy and psychological support in Rzeszow.
- Educational and psychoeducational offer.
- Supervision for professionals.
- Information about Renata Zuba's practice and professional profile.
